KNN

KNN as seen from above attempts to create boundaries for classification. In our case we are interested in creating two different boundaries as we are in a binary classification problem. This clustering technique attempts to find neighbors close to each other which is why distance is an important factor in our calculation. For further technical explanation and research click here.
